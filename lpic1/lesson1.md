### HARDWARE
- at first step, we should understand how you interact with your pc and what run your order!. pc is included of hardware such as cpu,gpu,NIC and so on. but how to interact with this shits? when you remove a image from your hard,what happens. there are three layer, OS>firmware>hardware. you interact with OS and os send message to firmware and the firmware interact with hardware. also each pc has a firmware, for example bios or ufi.
	- bios(basic input/output system)-> it's a old firmware that has a basic types of firmwares that boot the system with #MBR partition.
	- uefi(unified extensible firmware interface): it's a new that know as ufi and is more advanced and boot the system by /boot partition and FAT format that located in /boot directory in linux.
- NIC -> network interface card that let's you to connect internet throught wifi(wireless) or ethernet by network cable such as lan cable and rj45 connector and port LAN.
